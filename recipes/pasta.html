Pasta Alfredo Recipe

Description

Pasta Alfredo is a creamy and comforting Italian-inspired dish made with a rich sauce of butter, cream, and cheese. It is simple, quick to prepare, and perfect for an easy but satisfying meal.

This recipe is ideal for pasta lovers who enjoy smooth, cheesy sauces and can be customized with chicken or vegetables if desired.

Ingridients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Garlic
  • Parmesean cheese
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic and cook for a short time until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and let it heat gently.
  5. Add grated Parmesan cheese and stir until the sauce thickens.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and mix well.
  8. Serve hot with extra Parmesan cheese if desired.
