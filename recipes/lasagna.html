Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna is a comforting Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It is perfect for family meals or special occasions.

The recipe is simple to follow and results in a flavorful, hearty dish that can be prepared ahead of time and enjoyed by everyone.

Ingridients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Prehet the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets according to the package instructions
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook the onion and garlic until soft
  4. Add the ground beef and cook until browned
  5. Pour in the tomato sauce, season with salt and pepper, and simmer for a few minutes
  6. Prepare the béchamel sauce by melting butter, adding flour, and slowly mixing in milk.
  7. In a baking dish, layer pasta sheets, meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  9. Bake for 40-45 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  10. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
