Apple Salad Recipe

Description

Mexican sweet apple salad is a traditional and popular dish often served at family gatherings and celebrations. It combines sweet apples with creamy and fruity ingredients, creating a refreshing and comforting dessert-like salad.

This recipe is easy to prepare and is known for its balance of sweetness and texture, making it a favorite for all ages.

Ingridients

  • Apples
  • Pineapple canned, in small pieces
  • Raisins
  • Walnuts
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Sour cream or table cream

Steps

  1. Wash, peel, and chop the apples into small cubes.
  2. Drain the pineapple pieces and place them in a large bowl.
  3. Add the chopped apples, raisins, and walnuts to the bowl.
  4. Pour in the sweetened condensed milk.
  5. Add the sour cream or table cream.
  6. Mix everything gently until well combined.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  8. Serve cold.
